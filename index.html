Project Overview

The Food Donation Tracker aims to facilitate food donations from restaurants and grocery stores to local food banks, minimizing food waste and helping those in need.

Technologies Used

  • java script: Backend logic and user interface.
  • SQL: A database to store information about donors, food items, and food banks.

Features

  1. User Registration: Donors and food banks can register.
  2. Food Donation Entry: Donors can enter food items they wish to donate.
  3. View Donations: Food banks can view available food donations.
  4. Request Donations: Request for food items can be made by Food Bank and individuals.
